ABOUT THE AUTHOR

A teacher who dedicated a slice of her life to help a group of children grow up into responsible,reliable,compassionate and helpful adults

K T Margaret

Born on 1937 in Banglore || Did B.Sc at Maharani's College and B.Ed at MES college in Bangalore || Trained in understanding young children and their psychology || Worked as a teacher in one of the biggest slums in Bangalore for seven years || Trained teachers for NGOs working in rural areas of North Karnataka ||




THEME

LEARNING FOR LIFE is abook which will kindle the lamp of hope and faith in our minds,however deep the darkness around it is.

More exciting than any fiction,this real life narrative will give us deep insight into the minds of children reveling their will to fight against odds and the inborn goodness in them.One very valuable insight from the whole process of education is - no man is an island and cannot functionin isolation. The science of psychology gives one an insight and knowledge into human beings,their behaviour and the reasons for a particular behaviour. Mere intellectual knowledge about us will not help us get out of our behavioural problems and make a change in our life.
